Our Mission

RoboLoCo brings diverse, young intellects from all over Loudoun County together to compete in the FIRST Robotics Competition. We strive to inspire students and community members to advance in the professional and scientific world. Beyond competing in robotics competitions, we work to create a lasting impact on the community through outreach programs. We also aim to teach students professional skills not only Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math (STEM), but also in business and marketing. We exist for the inspiration and recognition of science and technology.
